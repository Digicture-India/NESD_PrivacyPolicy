Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how HomeworkNESD ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our school ERP system mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide our school management services:

  • Personal Information: Student names, parent/guardian contact details, teacher information, and academic records
  • Educational Data: Homework assignments, grades, attendance records, and academic progress
  • Communication Data: Messages between teachers, students, and parents within the app
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app usage analytics for performance improvement

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing school management and educational services
  • Facilitating communication between teachers, students, and parents
  • Tracking academic progress and attendance
  • Sending important notifications about school activities
  • Improving app functionality and user experience

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With authorized school personnel for educational purposes
  • With parents/guardians regarding their child's academic information
  • When required by law or to protect safety
  • With service providers who help us operate the app (under strict confidentiality agreements)

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encryption, secure servers, and regular security assessments.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for educational use and may collect information from children under 13 with proper school and parental consent. We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) requirements and only collect necessary educational information.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for educational purposes or as required by law. Academic records may be retained according to school policies and legal requirements.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal and educational requirements)
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes through the app or other appropriate means.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
