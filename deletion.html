Data Deletion Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2025

This Data Deletion Policy explains how users can request deletion of their personal data from HomeworkNESD and describes our data retention and deletion practices.

1. Your Right to Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data stored in our system. This includes:

  • Personal profile information
  • Communication records within the app
  • Non-essential academic data
  • Device and usage information

Important Note: Some educational records may be retained as required by law or school policy, even after a deletion request.

2. How to Request Data Deletion

Follow these steps to request data deletion:

Step 1: Contact your school administrator first to discuss your deletion request and understand any school-specific policies.
Step 2: Send a data deletion request to our support team at privacy@homeworknesd.com with the following information:
  • Your full name
  • School name
  • User ID or registered email address
  • Specific data you want deleted (or "all personal data")
  • Reason for deletion request
Step 3: We will verify your identity and process your request within 30 days.
Step 4: You will receive confirmation once the deletion process is complete.

3. What Data Cannot Be Deleted

Certain data may be retained even after a deletion request due to:

  • Legal requirements for educational record keeping
  • School policies requiring retention of academic records
  • Data needed for legitimate educational purposes
  • Information required for system security and fraud prevention
  • Data that has been anonymized and cannot be linked back to you

4. Automatic Data Deletion

We automatically delete certain types of data:

  • Temporary files and cache data: Deleted regularly
  • Session data: Deleted when you log out
  • Non-essential communication records: Deleted after 3 years
  • Device analytics: Deleted after 2 years

5. Account Deactivation vs. Data Deletion

Account Deactivation: Your account is disabled but data may be retained for school records.

Data Deletion: Personal data is permanently removed from our systems, subject to legal and educational requirements.

6. Deletion Process Timeline

Standard Timeline:

  • Request acknowledgment: Within 5 business days
  • Identity verification: 5-10 business days
  • Data deletion completion: Within 30 days of verified request
  • Confirmation notification: Within 2 business days of completion

7. Data Recovery After Deletion

Once data is deleted from our systems, it cannot be recovered. Please ensure you have copies of any important information before requesting deletion.

8. Third-Party Data

We will make reasonable efforts to request deletion of your data from third-party service providers we use, but we cannot guarantee deletion from all third-party systems.

9. Backup Data

Deleted data may remain in our backup systems for up to 90 days before being permanently purged during routine backup maintenance.

10. Minors' Data

For users under 18, data deletion requests must be made by a parent, guardian, or authorized school representative.

12. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Users will be notified of significant changes through the app or email.
